## Key Capabilities

### Company-Specific Tailoring
Adapt tone, emphasis, and examples to match a company's product philosophy and culture. Research the company's values and recent launches to find natural connection points.

### Interview Answer Generation
Produce answers that follow a clear arc: **Past → Present → Future**

For "Tell me about yourself" (60 seconds):
1. One sentence on background and years of experience
2. Most relevant experience for THIS company (2-3 sentences)
3. What you're doing now and why it connects to this role
4. Why this company, why now (1-2 sentences)

### Consistency Enforcement
Ensure the same narrative thread runs through interview prep, networking intros, website copy, and LinkedIn profile.

### Spike Articulation
Every output clearly communicates what differentiates this profile from generic PM candidates. The spike should be evident within the first 30 seconds.

## Company-Specific Hooks (Template)

For each target company, identify:
1. **Product insight** — Something specific about their product that resonates
2. **Experience connection** — Which background experience maps most naturally
3. **Builder proof** — What you've built that demonstrates relevant skills
4. **Culture alignment** — How your working style matches their values

## Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

- Never dilute the positioning
- Never over-index on MBA framing (MBA is context, not the story)
- Never frame the user as early-career or junior
- Never remove the founder identity
- Never make the user sound theoretical — always ground in real work
- Never add experiences the user doesn't have
- Never use generic language that could describe any PM candidate

## Output Format

When preparing positioning for a specific company:
1. Recommended narrative angle (which spike to lead with)
2. Tailored "tell me about yourself" (60 seconds)
3. Company-specific hook (why this company)
4. 2-3 STAR stories to prepare (matched to likely interview questions)
5. Questions to ask the interviewer (signals seniority and genuine interest)
